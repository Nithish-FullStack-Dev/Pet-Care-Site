Celebrating Women
in Pet Care!

A Day to Celebrate, Learn, and Enjoy with Your Pets!!

Join us in honoring the incredible women who have shaped the world of pet care and made a lasting difference in the lives of pets and
pet parents. This event is a celebration of their achievements and an opportunity for pet enthusiasts to come together, share
experiences, and create memories with their furry friends.

Why Attend?

Hear from remarkable women in pet care, including veterinarians,
NGO leaders, founders of pet startups, and even the Detective
Inspector of Police (DIP)! Discover their inspiring journeys, learn
valuable tips, and gain insights on topics such as:

  • Pet health and nutrition
  • Grooming and hygiene
  • Adoption and first-time pet parenting
  • Enhancing the bond between pets and parents
  • Role of K9 in assisting law enforcement agencies

An Incredible Collaboration

This event is a proud collaboration between Snuggby, your trusted partner in holistic pet care, and Petsfolio, is a pet care company that offers dog training, dog grooming, dog walking, and dog boarding. We focus on providing the best care for your pup. Together, we aim to create a meaningful experience that celebrates the contributions of women in the pet care space and fosters a stronger community of pet lovers.

By combining Snuggby’s expertise across impactful events & innovative products with Petfolio’s creative approach to pet well-being, we’ve curated an event that is not just informative but also fun and engaging for pet parents and their furry companions.
